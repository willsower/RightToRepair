AS A COMMUNITY WE CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE

Pushing for repair freedom is crucial for the right to repair. For too long have manufacturers been able to monopolize and drive prices up as the quality of their products decrease. With these unfair practices, we need to hold them accountable and fight back for what is right, your rights.


Educate the Community

When something breaks, the majority of users throw the product out and get a new one. Step 1 when a product breaks should be to repair.


Push for Sustainability

Only a small percentage of electronics are actually recycled. Resulting in toxins polluting drinking water and land.


Repair Freedom

Your rights should be fought for, we should not have to bend over backwords to the manufacturers.

"Would you buy a car if it was illegal to replace the tires?"
